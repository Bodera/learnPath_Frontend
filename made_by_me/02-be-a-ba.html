Opening tags indicates that anything between it and the near correlated closing tag has a especifical importance and need to be interpreted as a proper element. They start with a < and finish with a >.

Attributes provide additional information about the contents of an element. They appear on the opening tag of the element and are made up of two parts: an attribute name and it's value.
They main purpose is to indicates what kind of extra information you are suppyling about the element's content. By conveniance, it should be written in lowercase.


The values are the information or a setting for the attribute, and they always are placed in double quotes.
